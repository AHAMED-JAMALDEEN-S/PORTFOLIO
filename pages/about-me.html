About Me

I am Ahamed Jamaldeen and would like to be called ‘Jamal’. I am from Trichy, Tamil Nadu, India.

2017 - 2019

I remember during my high school, the only subject I was interested in was computer science. Not that I hate all other subjects, but it was the only subject that I could understand fully. Even solving small problems (addition of two numbers, etc.) gave me huge satisfaction. That was when I decided that this had to be my career, and I want to enjoy the satisfaction of solving problems till I retire.

2019 - 2023

Then I went to college, choosing my favourite subject as my career path, dreaming of getting the same satisfaction which I got during my school life. But then COVID hit; everything went online—my classes, semester exams—and I completed almost 50% of my degree online without even knowing the context. When we returned to college during the 6th semester, the placements had already started. Almost 90% of my class got placed at the end of college, and I was in the remaining 10%. I completed my degree without any knowledge or skills, and all I had was myself to blame.

2023 - 2024

This was the hardest phase of my life, where I had a degree without any skills. Wherever there were hirings, almost 2,000 to 3,000 people showed up for close to 15 vacancies, and I could not even go past the initial round. So, I decided to start everything from scratch, started building technical skills by doing small projects. Finally, after almost a year of hard work and patience, I got my first offer in Trichy.
