About Me

2017 - 2019

During my high school, computer science was the one subject that truly resonated with me. I didn’t dislike the others, but this was the only field I could completely understand and genuinely enjoy. Even solving the simplest problems gave me a sense of accomplishment. That feeling of satisfaction made me realize that problem-solving through code was what I wanted to pursue for the rest of my life.

2019 - 2023

When I entered college, I chose computer science with the hope of continuing that same passion and excitement. However, the pandemic changed everything. Classes and examinations moved online, and a significant portion of my degree was completed without proper context or hands-on learning. By the time we returned to campus, placement drives had already begun. While most of my classmates secured jobs, I was among the few who didn’t. I graduated realizing that I lacked both confidence and practical skills, and there was no one else to hold responsible but myself.

2023 - 2024

This phase was the most challenging period of my journey. I had a degree but no real skills to back it up. Job openings were scarce, competition was intense, and I struggled to clear even the initial rounds. Instead of giving up, I made the decision to start over from the basics. I began building my technical foundation step by step, working on small projects and learning through consistent practice. With time, patience, and perseverance, my efforts paid off, and I finally secured my first role as a software professional in Trichy.
